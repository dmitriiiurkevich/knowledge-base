Три стандартных потока:
- 0 — stdin
- 1 — stdout
- 2 — stderr


`$ cat my_file_1 my_file_2 my_file_3`
`$ tac my_file_1 my_file_2 my_file_3`

`$ printf "line 1\nline 2\n" > my_file`
`$ printf "line 1\nline 2\n" >> my_file`

`$ printf "line 1\nline 2\n" &> my_file`
`$ printf "line 1\nline 2\n" &>> my_file`

`$ printf "line 1\nline 2\n" 2> my_file`
`$ printf "line 1\nline 2\n" 2>> my_file`

## Виды перенаправлений стандартных потоков

`$ echo "hello world" > my_file`

`$ ls . | cat`

`$ cat < my_file`
